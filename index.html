A workshop to design the Vera C. Rubin Observatory LSST Target of Opportunity program

This workshop will bring together members of the community with scientific interest and knowledge in Targets of Opportunity to make recommendations to the Survey Cadence Optimization Committee of LSST.

  • Next

Rational

The Rubin LSST survey cadence is designed in an interative community driven process (Bianco+2021). The implementation of a Target of Opportunity program usuing up to 3% of the LSST sky time has been recommanded by the Survey Cadence Optimization Committee (SCOC) (PSTN-055). This program is recommanded to initially respond to Gravitationl Wave triggers, but with the potential to expand to other phenomena of interest. This workshop is designed to bring together experts from the scientific community, Rubin Observatory personnel, and member of the Rubin SCOC to deliver a recommendation for the implementation of the ToO program including: how to select and prioritize triggers and automate response to triggers, what images and cadence is required for triggers. The workshop is designed with plenary discussion to define the boundary conditions for the ToO set by the observatorty, and collaborative breakouts to define the neads of identifice scientific cases and develop definitions of triggers and prioritization schemes.
